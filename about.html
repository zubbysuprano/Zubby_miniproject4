WHO WE ARE


WELL-FITNESS GYM & WELLNESS CENTRE


WELL-Fitness is a health and wellness company. With 21 branches across Nigeria, our highly equipped gym facilities complemented by the expertise of over 250 professionally certified personal trainers provide our members with a holistic and all-inclusive fitness and wellness experience. Our offerings include multi-location access and over 50 varied monthly fitness programs to suit and support the fitness lifestyles of our vast membership base. WELL-Fitness is committed to providing a youthful, entertaining yet rewarding fitness experience to many thousands of people, thereby promoting access to happier and healthier lives. WELL-Fitness is managed by a Board of Directors comprising seasoned professionals with extensive years of experience and a range of diverse specialities that complement the growth and development of the company.

Femi Ade-John

Femi is the founder and CEO of WELL-Fitness Gym and Wellness Centre Ltd. He is a serial entrepreneur with several years of experience in Sales, Marketing and HR with Fly Bank Plc. He began his journey into entrepreneurship in 2013. He founded Premiun Influence Ltd in 2011, which later became JET Mobile in 2015 – an electronics retail chain with 5 branches in Lagos. The company was the first to launch two innovative retail initiatives in Nigeria in mobile: the mobile electronics insurance with Eve Havard PLC and the mobile swap initiative enabling customers to trade in their old devices (laptops & phones) for new ones. He successfully led the company from an annual turnover of N400m in 2011 to N4bn annual turnover in 2016 before entering into an acquisition deal with Modala Nigeria Ltd, now Yumga Ltd. Femi founded WELL-Fitness in 2015 with the vision to build Africa’s preferred fitness club.

Josh Alfred

Josh is a Co-founder and Managing Partner of Meteor. Before founding Meteor, Josh held roles at Sunbank Nigeria, Lake Holdings, and Kings & Company. He is a Director of JPTA Apparel Canada. Josh also sits on the board of the African Events Capital Forum (AECF) and the Private Equity and Venture Capital Association of Nigeria (PEVCAN). He was named a Young Global Leader by the World Economic Forum in 2014. He received a B.Sc. in Electrical Engineering from Calvin College, an M.Sc. in Engineering-Economic Systems & Operations Research from Stanford University, and an MBA from Harvard Business School

Peter Maddison

Peter co-founded Blue Block Road, a creative solutions company in 2010 and is currently Executive Director Strategy & Innovations. He has over 35 years of experience in marketing and business strategy experience, spanning 5 continents, and a variety of creative sectors including advertising, experiential marketing, content production and distribution, and pay TV. He has worked with McTom London, rising to the position of Vice President and co-founding IdeaMedia, a lifestyle marketing practice group.

Jennifer Watson

Jennifer is an independent Director of Meteor Capital. She has extensive experience in C Suite, consumer insights, business and marketing strategies, and campaign design and execution. She was Marketing Director at Standard Bank Group South Africa and later in Nigeria, and has worked with Kantar East Africa, Kenya where she consulted for Google and Coke. She was invited to Nigeria and became the Marketing Director at Chiffon Ricard Nigeria where the Peterson and Cartell brands became marketing leaders during her tenure. Jennifer has a degree in Mathematics and Psychology, a post-graduate degree in Psychology and in Education, and an MBA.
